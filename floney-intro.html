About Floney

Floney is a collaborative household budget management application that enables families, couples, and groups to track and manage their shared expenses together in real-time. Since launching in April 2023, we've been helping users gain transparency and control over their shared finances.

System Architecture

Our production-grade architecture is designed for scalability, reliability, and performance:

  • Database Replication: High availability and disaster recovery
  • Caching Layer: Redis for improved performance
  • RESTful API Design: Clean and documented APIs
  • OAuth2 Integration: Secure social login (Google, Apple)
  • Comprehensive Testing: Unit and integration tests with JUnit
